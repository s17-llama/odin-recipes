Tiramisu

Description

This tiramisu cake tastes fancy without all the work. This cake is wonderful for a get-together or special occasion at home. Using a boxed cake mix as a base is a real time-saver!

LOOKS DELICIOUS TO EAT!

Ingredients

  • Cake
    • Cake mix
    • Water
    • Egg whites
    • Oil
    • Coffee Powder
  • Coffee Syrup
    • Coffee
    • Coffee flavored liquor
  • Filling
    • Mascarpone cheese
    • Sugar
    • Coffee flavored liquor
  • Frosting
    • Heavy Cream
    • Confectioners' sugar
    • 2 tablespoons coffee flavored liquor
  • Garnish
    • Cocoa powder
    • Semisweet chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Make cake.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of cakes comes out clean.
  4. Make syrup.
  5. Make filling.
  6. Make frosting.
